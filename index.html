Numbergang is a rap group gaining prestige and reputation in South Carolina. Recently they have been doing shows outside of SC to gain more popularity. CP, B-Rad, and DJ Famouz are rising in the rap industry and they have hopes of becoming big time artists one day but they can only get there with the help of you the fans so subscribe to this page so we can give you updates on the latest and hottest things going on with Numbergang.

Group Members

Chris Petty aka CP from Forest City, NC is the co-leader of Numbergang.

Brad Nelson aka B-Rad from Spartanburg, SC is the other co-leader of Numbergang.

DJ Famouz from Spartanburg, SC is the DJ of the group he controls anything that has to do with the beats used in songs.
